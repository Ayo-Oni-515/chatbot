Manage your availability
To set your available days and time:

Visit the My Subjects page

On this page you can manage your availability by selecting "Manage Availability" by the right side of the page

Now you can manage your Availability

A friendly reminder: Do not forget to check and change your selected timezone if needed. Please choose the days you are available for your teaching. This covers all your subjects. This is what would be shown to the potential students.
