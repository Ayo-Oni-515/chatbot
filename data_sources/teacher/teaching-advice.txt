Teaching advice

1. Have a clear outline of the course and each Class

Clear Course Outline: Develop a clear outline for the entire course and each class to set expectations and ensure organization. Over-deliver to exceed expectations.

2. Plan different activities to keep students focused 

Diverse Activities: Plan varied activities to keep students engaged and encourage learning. Introduce surprises like games or competitions to maintain motivation.

3. Get the right equipment

Get Quality Equipment: Invest in good, budget-friendly equipment, especially a reliable microphone. Regularly test and update your equipment list for optimal performance.

4. Make sure they are keeping up with the course

Encourage Course Engagement: Implement incentives, deadlines, and due dates to motivate students to keep up with the course. Use tools like Google Calendar for effective scheduling and reminders.
