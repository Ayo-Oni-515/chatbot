Updating my Video, Pictures and Profile

To update your video and picture, you need to click on the Account Setting and you will see the Account Setting page. here you can adjust virtually any parameter connected to your teacher account.

To update your images or picture, go to the picture section and click and update. Same for the videos. You have 3 options for video upload: 1. Upload video, 2. record video from browser, 3. Add YouTube link, (NB: Only Youtube link permitted).

Please remember that your video size must not exceed 100MB and not more than 300 seconds. You will not be able to upload larger than 100MB on our platform for now.
