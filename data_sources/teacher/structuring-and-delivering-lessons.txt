Structuring and Delivering Lessons
Structuring and delivering lessons

1. Clear Objectives: Define clear lesson objectives and communicate its real-world relevance to students, using examples.

2. Model Expectations: Demonstrate and explain expectations, especially for hands-on activities, ensuring proper understanding.

3. Active Engagement: Foster student participation through hands-on activities, cooperative learning, or technology to achieve the lesson objective.

4. Be Mobile and Supportive: Move around the classroom, addressing questions, providing reminders, and using critical thinking questions to reinforce comprehension.

Note that this structuring can be used for various levels 

5.Positive Reinforcement: Acknowledge and compliment students for positive behavior and hard work, creating a positive learning environment.
