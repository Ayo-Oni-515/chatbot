How do I reset my password?
To reset your password:

1. Go to the sign in page.

2. Select "Forgot password".  

3. Input your Email address on the dialogue box, shown on the following page.  

 4. Follow the instructions sent to your email via a password reset link.  

 Note: Passwords are confidential, and we do not have access to or share them. Two-factor authorization (2FA) is implemented for enhanced security. For assistance, visit our help cente
