Why can't I proceed with my registration?

To troubleshoot registration issues on Educify:
1. Check Required Fields: Ensure all mandatory fields are correctly filled.

2. Verify Email and Username: Confirm validity and uniqueness.

3. Password Compliance: Meet platform's password requirements.

4. Browser Compatibility: Use a compatible and updated browser.

5. Clear Cache and Cookies: Clear browser data or use incognito mode.

6. Network Connectivity: Ensure a stable internet connection.

7. Contact Support: Seek assistance from Educify's support team.

8. Explore Alternatives: Consider alternative registration methods if available.
