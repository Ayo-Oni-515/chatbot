How do I deactivate my Account from Educify?

To terminate, suspend, or delete your Educify.org account:

Access your teacher profile.
Indicate unavailability for new students or contact customer support via the help center. Use the teacher account setting.

Follow the provided steps or speak to a representative for assistance. You can all us on +1 8882529485 and send email to support@educify.org and we shall delete the account for you.
