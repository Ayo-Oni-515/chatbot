Teaching Calendar & Its features

In the teacher Calendar tab, you can  check your schedules and control your availability. You can also mark attendance of all your lessons. Please note that, you cannot mark attendance of future lessons.

Setting your availability: You can set your availability for teaching here. Please note, if you want to set your actual availability, you have to go to the "Listing" section.

You can also see and know when you will be having classes, to avoid missing your classes; to plan ahead along with your roaster.
