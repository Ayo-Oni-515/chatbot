How do I update or change my Password?

To change your Password or update it, you go to the Account Settings page and scroll to the bottom of the page and you will see that options there.

WHAT IF I CANNOT LOGIN?:
If you cannot login, you have to click on "Forgot Password' option on the login page; (See the image below)

You will see this page for resetting the password: Enter your email address and you will get an email for changing your password.
