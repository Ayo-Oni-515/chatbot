How do I add a new lesson?
 You can do this by clicking the 'My Listings' option  

Then the 'Add new lesson' button', as below;

This takes you to the 'Add new course' page,

Now you will have to complete the form on this page to add a lesson

You can now enter your course details, complete the course information, add videos and images, then click 'Save Lesson'. 

Congratulations! You just created a lesson.
