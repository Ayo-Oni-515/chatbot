How do I see all the lessons my students have scheduled?

To find your scheduled lessons, check in the "Upcoming Appointments" section. You see this immediately you login and it is hard to miss.

You can also see your schedule from the Calendar section. You will see the upcoming lessons on the calendar in blue colour as shown in the picture.
