Avoid Inconsistencies in lessons
To avoid inconsistencies;

▪️ Clearly outline learning objectives

▪️ Maintain a structured lesson plan

▪️ Use diverse teaching methods

▪️ Provide timely and constructive feedback

▪️ Adapt to students' needs and pace

▪️ Encourage active student participation

▪️ Stay organized and well-prepared

▪️ Foster a positive and inclusive learning environment

▪️ Utilize technology effectively

▪️ Continuously assess and adjust your teaching approach
