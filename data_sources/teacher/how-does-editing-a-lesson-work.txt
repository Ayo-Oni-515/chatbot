How does editing a Lesson work?

Edit or reschedule lessons through the "My Listing" menu option.

 Navigate to your teacher dashboard, select the desired lesson from the list, and click on the subject for editing or rescheduling.

After editing the lesson, you need to click the save button below the page to save the edition.
