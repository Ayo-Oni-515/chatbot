Updating Your Education and Certification

You can update your Education and Certification through your teacher Dashboard after logging in. Follow this step to update your Education and certification:

Log in with your credentials (Username & Password):
Click on your profile picture to the top RH corner, you will see your 'Account Settings" and sign out options under your picture:
Click on the "Account Settings" options and you will see the Account Settings page. Here you can adjust all you inputs you added while signing up as Teacher.
 Scroll down to the Certification and Education section of the "Account Settings" page and update your education and certification and other information.
