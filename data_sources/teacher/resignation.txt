Resignation
RESIGNATION:

To ensure a smooth transition and adequate time for us to find a suitable replacement, teachers are required to provide at least 15 days' notice prior to their resignation.

Procedure for Resignation Notification:

Resignations must be officially communicated via email.
The resignation email should be sent to: hr@educify.org
Important Note:

Any staff member who fails to provide proper notice of resignation, and is deemed to have abandoned their position, will have their outstanding payments withheld.
