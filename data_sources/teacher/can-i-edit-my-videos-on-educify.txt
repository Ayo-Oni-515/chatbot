Can I edit my videos on Educify?

The question is: does Educify have video editing capability? No, You cannot edit your videos on educify, at the moment. You can, however, record a video using our platform or upload a video or copy and paste a Youtube video link. Note that, the only link allowed is a Youtube video link.

You can use the switch as shown above to choose between uploading a video/ recording a video and adding a video link( only Youtube link allowed)
