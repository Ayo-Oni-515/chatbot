What is the recommended duration for lessons

Generally, aim for a duration that allows for comprehensive coverage without overwhelming learners.

The recommended duration for lessons on Educify can vary based on the content and target audience.

Many successful lessons fall within the range of 30 minutes to 1 hour, but it ultimately depends on the complexity of the material and the preferences of your audience. 

Consider breaking down longer content into manageable sections or providing supplementary materials for deeper exploration. 

Additionally, gathering feedback from learners can help you fine-tune the ideal duration for your specific lessons.
