How do I upload a lesson?

You can do this by clicking the 'Add new lesson' option on the My listings page 

On the next page, you would be able to add your lesson details"

Complete the course information, and click 'Save Lesson' box below the information page. 

Finally your lessons should be displayed as in the first image above (ukelele 1a & b) and you just uploaded a lesson
