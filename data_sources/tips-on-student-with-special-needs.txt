Tips on students with special needs

1.  Limit Distraction: Use personalized content like alphabet boards and flashcards to engage children with special needs and minimize distractions

2. Utilize Music and Voice Inflection: Incorporate songs, varied voice inflections, and animal sounds to stimulate brain development and foster a strong teacher-student relationship.

3.Break Down Instructions: Simplify instructions into smaller, manageable tasks using both verbal and written cues, promoting better understanding and reducing mistakes.

4.Provide Adequate Breaks: Keep lessons interesting by incorporating activities like games, yoga, and storybooks. Offer regular breaks to prevent boredom and allow children to relax and refocus.
