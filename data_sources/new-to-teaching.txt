New to teaching?
New to teaching and new to Educify Platform? 

We have different categories of teachers. if you are new, you belong to the "New" teacher category.  Here are some checklists of what you should be aware of as a new teacher.

Clear Objective: Define lesson objectives and explain real-world relevance with examples.
Model Expectations: Teach and demonstrate expectations, including consequences, before the lesson.
Active Engagement: Engage students with hands-on activities, cooperative learning, or technology.
Be Mobile: Move around the classroom, answering questions, giving reminders, and asking critical thinking questions.
Positive Reinforcement: Compliment students for positive behavior and hard work.
Reflect and Improve: After meeting the objective, reflect on lesson effectiveness, identify patterns, seek input, and continuously improve teaching skills.
