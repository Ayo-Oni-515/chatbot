How can I make my lessons fun?

1. Break Up Lessons: Keep students engaged by alternating between lecture and activities, incorporating breaks and varying teaching methods.

2. Offer Choices: Empower students by providing choices in activities or homework, fostering engagement and decision-making skills.

3. Utilize Games: Integrate educational games into lessons for instant engagement and a more enjoyable learning experience.

4. Embrace Technology: Incorporate technology tools, apps, and videos to make learning familiar, fun, and aligned with real-world skills.

5. Add Mystery: Create anticipation and interest by incorporating mystery into lessons, revealing clues leading up to new topics or activities.
