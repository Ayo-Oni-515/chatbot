How do I improve my lesson title?
We just have some suggestions on how you can get a better chance of landing the perfect student and best student for you. This can simply be done by improving your lesson title. Random titles that have no correlation with your subject impact your visibility very badly.

Here are a few suggestions on improving  your lesson title:
Ensure your title is short: This should be like 4 -6 words max.
Ensure it contains your subject. Examples are: English lesson for Beginner, Maths lesson for adults. Make it very relevant to your subject and target audience.
