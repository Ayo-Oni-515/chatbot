Having issues with mic and Camera in online lessons?
Sometimes, you may be having issues enabling the video Camera and microphone for the 8x8 our partner.

Here are the steps for Chrome and Brave mobile browsers:

Go to settings and then choose site settings;

Open and enable site settings and you want to make sure you enable permission for camera and microphone.

Enable the permission and remove educify from the block list of the sites.

Next, make educify an allowed website and click site preferences.

Next, click allow:
