What is a Lesson Credit?

Lesson credit is the total cost of the lessons you have subscribed for or have in your cart, or you have liked and would want to take. You can find this under your profile and associated with the teachers showing up under the lesson section of your student profile. 
Lesson credits can be used towards payment for new lessons.A lesson credit refers to the total cost of an individual lesson on your calendar. Lesson credits vary based on your teacher's prices for lessons, the length of lessons, and lesson location.
