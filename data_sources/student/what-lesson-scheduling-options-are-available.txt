What lesson scheduling options are available?

Educify offers two major types of scheduling options: Fixed and Pay-as-you-go.

The fixed schedule can be weekly or fortnightly (lesson every other week). This scheduling options guarantees that you have a reserved schedule with your teacher.

The pay-as-you-go option is the flexible option of scheduling lesson when it is convenient for you. This option is more expensive and it depends on the availability of the teacher.  you can reach us through the helpcenter to ask for these options;.
