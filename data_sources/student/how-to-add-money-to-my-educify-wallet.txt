How to add money to my educify wallet

What is Educify wallet? The wallet is saving account on the platform, the funds in which you can use to purchase lessons, items and courses. It works as good as credit card or other payment methods.

To credit your wallet, all you need to do is to go to "My wallet" and then click on the "add to wallet " button.

It then gives you the option of adding credit with credit card or PayPal.

Please note that only credit card and PayPal are available on the the website as a means of payment
