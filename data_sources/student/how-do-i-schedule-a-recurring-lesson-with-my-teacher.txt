How do I schedule a recurring lesson with my teacher?
To schedule a recurring lesson with your teacher, you have to book a package and choose the recurring package" option. You can also find this on your dashboard. 

By choosing the "recurring lessons" option, you are agreeing to our terms of service and cancellation and suspension policies. We require at least 12 hrs prior notice for cancellation of a lesson and 1 month's notice for suspension or termination of the Subscription.
