How to pay for lessons and courses on educify

To be able to pay for all lessons and courses on educify platform, all you need do is to go to "payment method" on dashboard and then click on it. We currently accept credit card and Paypal as methods of payment.

It then shows you a page which involves all details about payment, and also means of payment which is by the top right corner of the page.

You are to choose your preferred payment type.
