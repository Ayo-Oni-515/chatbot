How to sign up on educify

In order to sign up on educify, that implies that you already have an account with us. 

Go to our site " educify.org " making use of any browse of your choice.
At the top left corner of the site, you'll find a three tag there which you must click.
After then, you'll find a sign in button there; just click on it.
It then takes the student to the page where you'll sign in. 
Click on " sign in  with Google".
Select the Gmail account used in signing in previously.

Welcome back to educify!!
