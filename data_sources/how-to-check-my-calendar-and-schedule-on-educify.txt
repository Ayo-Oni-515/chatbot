How to check for my calendar and schedule on educify

To be able to get and set your own convenient, and best schedules as a student on educify, you will need to just follow these simple steps :

Go to the dashboard and click on the calendar icon.
It then takes you to where you'll find all the classes you will be attending, as well as the time and date schedules. 

In addition to this, you can also reschedule your classes by just restructuring your calendar options and schedules. You can also edit your availability as a student on the calendar icon, and also check for upcoming classes. 

You can also view your teacher's availability, and schedules as it will show up in the calendar box
