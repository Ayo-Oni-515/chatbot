How does my profile look to students?

The look and feel of your profile is based on the design of our teacher listing pages. It is important that you make your profile very attractive to student. Add as much information as possible to enable them make the decision to try you out or better still, book with you. Here are some important issues to take note of:

Add a very professional profile picture
Make the title of your listing compelling and SEO friendly
Add video - although optional, it makes it easier to sell
Add location photos, in case you teach at your location.
Ensure to keep your availability updated
Ensure your listing Description has all the keywords that make it easier for your to be found.
