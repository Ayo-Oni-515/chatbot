Logging in and reseting your password
How do I log into my Teacher Account?

1. Log in at https://educify.org/#/login with your username and password.

How do I reset my password?

2. If you forgot password? Click "Forgot Password"  on the sign in page,

3. On the next page input your email in the dialogue box provided. A link will be sent to your email to reset your password.

For username issues, contact customer support.
